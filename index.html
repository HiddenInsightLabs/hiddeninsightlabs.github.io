Careful testing for augmented reality and immersive systems

Hidden Insight Labs is an independent testing practice focused on identifying real-world risks in AR, spatial, and emerging interactive technologies before they reach users.

What testing reveals

Across immersive projects, the most significant risks are rarely visual fidelity. They emerge from interaction breakdowns, environmental constraints, and mismatches between system behavior and user expectations.

How early testing reduces risk

AR systems fail in predictable stages. Starting testing early allows teams to remove the most damaging issues first, then progressively refine the experience.

Our perspective

Immersive systems behave differently outside controlled environments. Lighting, space, movement, hardware limitations, and user expectations all introduce failure modes that are easy to miss during development.
